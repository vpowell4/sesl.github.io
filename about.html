About SESL

Our Mission

To bring deterministic, transparent, explainable reasoning to automated decision systems.

Founders

Vince Powell has spent over 25 years working in businesses developing, delivering and using technology to grow businesses and make them more efficent. Unusally while holding senior business rules, Vince continues to be deeply engaged with the technology. Fustrated with the lack of transparency and explainability in AI systems, and concerned with the effort involved in trying to them to answer correctly. Vince founded SESL to create a solution that prioritises clarity and accountability in automated decision-making.

Roadmap

We are at the start of our journey. Having developed the core rules engine, the user interface (the CLI and the MCP server) and run over 10,000 tests we want to get the technology out to people to use and develop industry specific solutions.

We don't plan to change the engine or the language (other than bug fixes), but do see value in having more tools to manage the rules and scenarios, and improving the presentation of the rule tracing and fact usage.

We would be delighted to hear from you if you have any requests, suggestions or even just comments !

Who are we ?

SESL is part of a group of solutions produced and managed by the Oblongix business. Oblongix was established to provide advanced business analytical solutions. Our team has deep technical and business expertise in data analytics, business rules management, decision automation, and software development across many industry sectors and business processes. Through our qualifications, experience and relationships we are able to bring powerful capabilities to solving complex (and sometimes "unsolveable") problems. We are passionate about creating tools that empower businesses to make better, informed, transparent decisions through technology.
