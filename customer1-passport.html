Automating Passport Eligibility

A government agency used SESL to convert decades of legal rules into a deterministic, fully auditable eligibility engine.

The Challenge

The rules governing passport eligibility were spread across outdated documents, inconsistent internal notes, and embedded logic in legacy systems. Staff frequently disagreed on interpretations, and audits were slow and expensive.

Why SESL?

  • 100% deterministic decision-making
  • Full explanation of every rule applied
  • Forward-chaining engine matches legal reasoning
  • Easy for internal policy teams to author rules

Impact

After migrating to SESL, processing time dropped, appeals reduced, and internal trust in decisions increased dramatically.

  • 38% faster eligibility checks
  • 70% fewer decision disputes
  • 100% auditor satisfaction due to rule tracing
