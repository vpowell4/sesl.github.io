Transforming Policy into a Living Rule Engine

A regulatory organisation used SESL to convert thousands of pages of policy into a continuously updated, fully auditable logic engine.

The Challenge

Policy changes frequently, but internal systems were slow to update. Teams relied on manual interpretations, FAQs, email threads, and spreadsheets, leading to inconsistent results and risks during audits.

How SESL Helped

  • Policy teams turned written guidance into deterministic SESL rules
  • Automatic rule linking ensured no contradictions
  • Scenario testing validated every policy update instantly
  • Explainability views provided auditor-ready trace evidence

Impact

  • 94% reduction in policy interpretation disputes
  • Real-time propagation of policy updates
  • Zero errors found in external audits
  • Cross-department alignment on rule meaning
